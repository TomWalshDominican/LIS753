Friends of Palo Alto Library

Visit our monthly sale!

What you'll find

  • Books - hardcovers and paperbacks, in all genres, for all ages!
  • Magazines - vintage and recent; includes comic books!
  • Music - records, CDs, cassettes
  • Videos - Blu-Rays, DVDs, and VHS
  • Toys - including STEM kits!

Discover over 50,000 treasures! Most items are $1 or less. We have materials in English, Spanish, French, and Chinese. This is a great opportunity for teachers, parents, collectors, and everyone!
